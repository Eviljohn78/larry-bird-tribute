Larry Bird

The hick from French Lick

Larry Bird along with his teammates and team owner Red Auerbach after winning the NBA Championship in 1981.

Timeline of Larry Bird's life in basketball:

  • 1956 - Born on December 7th in West Baden, Indiana
  • 1971 - Plays basketball at Springs Valley High School in French Lick, Indiana
  • 1975 - Plays basketball at Indiana State University in Terre Haute, Indiana
  • 1979
    • John Wooden Collegiate Player of the Year
    • Signs his first contract with the Boston Celtics
  • 1980 - Named NBA Rookie of the Year
  • 1981 - Boston Celtics win the NBA Championship
  • 1984
    • Boston Celtics win the NBA Championship
    • NBA Player of the Year
  • 1985 - NBA Player of the Year
  • 1986
    • Boston Celtics win the NBA Championship
    • NBA Player of the Year
  • 1992
    • Retires from professional basketball
    • Wins an Olympic Gold Medal as a member of the Dream Team
  • 1997 - Becomes coach of the Indiana Pacers
  • 1998
    • NBA Coach of the Year
    • Inducted into the Basketball Hall of Fame
  • 2003 - Becomes President of Basketball Operations for the Indiana Pacers
  • 2012 - NBA Executive of the Year
